Hello, I'm Marie Almasy!

Welcome to Sunshine And More! I'm a self-taught polymer clay artist based in Michigan. My love for creating unique, handmade items began as a hobby and quickly grew into a passion for sharing my art with the world.

My Artistic Journey

It all started with a simple set of clay tools and a desire to express myself. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed a style that blends whimsy and sophistication. From delicate earrings to intricate bookmarks, every piece tells a story and is made with love.

When I'm not working on my next collection, I love exploring nature for inspiration, spending time with my family, and connecting with fellow artists and art lovers like you.

Fun Facts About Me

  • I’ve been crafting with polymer clay for over 5 years.
  • I find inspiration in sunsets, wildlife, and the vibrant colors of nature.
  • I’m a coffee enthusiast and believe creativity pairs best with a cup of java.
  • Every piece I make is unique—no two are ever exactly alike!
